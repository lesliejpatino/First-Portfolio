Hey there, I'm Leslie!

B A L A N C E ... a fitting word that describes the why behind most choices that I make.
I like to think logically as much I like to imagine & create. I love routine as much as I crave change.

A majority of my career has been in event planning and management - two fields which require loads of problem solving, logic, creativity and patience. The key factor that I found missing was that no matter what, there was hardly an objectively correct answer or solution. Ultimately, results were always slightly subjective and I longed for something that provided results that were more black and white, without sacrificing my desire for creative expression.

Here is where website development came into the picture & I couldn't be happier about this transition on a personal and professional level. Please, stick around and take a look at some of the work I've done so far!
